Ingredients

  1. Gehun
  2. Hara Masala
  3. Hara Dhaniya
  4. Lehsen
  5. Hari Mirch
  6. Gosht
  7. Salt
  8. Adrak lehsen

Preperation

  1. Boil gehunn bamix it
  2. Prepare hara masala ...hara dhania, lehsen n hari mirch
  3. Boil gosht adding namak n adrak lehsen... keep extra water
  4. Baghar with Kari patta, pyaz, hara masala n add boiled gehun n gosht...add water as required
