Ingredients

  1. Full Chicken
  2. Adrak Lehsan Paste
  3. Lemon Juice
  4. Green Chilli Paste
  5. Salt
  6. Tomato sauce
  7. Dahi
  8. Lehsen
  9. Oil
  10. Chilli Garlic
  11. Maida
  12. Milk

  16. Capsicum
  17. Carrots

  19. Vinegar
  20. Sugar

Preperation

  1. Full Chicken Marinate with adrak lehsen paste, lemon juice, green chili paste, salt and keep it overnight for good results.
  2. Put it in a saucepan n let it cook on slow flame, turn it once. Once colour changes, remove from flame. Keep the sage aside.
  3. Then fry it in oil and keep aside. Boil macaroni and keep aside.
  4. For Red Sauce: take tomato sauce, dahi, lehsen, oil, salt and add Chilli Garlic too, if you want and blend it
  5. White Sauce: maida, milk, salt, oregano, lehsen, barik pyaz and make it as you make for Lasagne.
  6. White Sauce: maida, milk, salt, oregano, lehsen, barik pyaz and make it as you make for Lasagne.
  7. Assemble the veggies n chicken in a dish and grill it in the oven.
  8. For Green Sauce: Blend green chili with sarka n lehsen n zeera.
