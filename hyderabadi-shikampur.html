Ingredients

  1. 1/2 Kgs Mutton
  2. 100gms Chana Daal
  3. 1tb sp. Salt
  4. 4 Green Chillies
  5. 1 Big Chopped Onion
  6. 1 tb sp Red Chilli Powder
  7. 1 tb sp Garam Masala
  8. 3 tb sp Adrak Lehsen Paste
  9. 1/2 tb sp Haldi
  10. 2 tb sp oil
  11. Handful Green Dhaniya
  12. Handful Pudina
  13. 1 Glass Water
  14. Curd

Preperation

  1. Cook in pressure cooker till dry.
  2. Add dry powdered, coconut kaju, badam chironji, qash qash, limejuice 1 tb sp, 3 tb sp dahin mix n grind mutton.
