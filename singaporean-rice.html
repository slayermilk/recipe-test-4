Ingredients

  1. Chicken Boneless
  2. Qeema for kabab
  3. Capsicum
  4. Hari Pyaz
  5. Normal Pyaz, cut into 4 pieces
  6. Gajar
  7. Corn
  8. Band Ghobi
  9. Mayonnaise
  10. Dahi
  11. Chilli Flakes
  12. Tomato without Skin
  13. Salt
  14. Sugar
  15. Chilli Garlic Sauce

Preperation

  1. Boil rice like this for biryani n add star anise.
  2. Marinate chicken with soya sauce, worcestershire sauce, chilly flakes, salt, sugar, tomato sauce, chili garlic accordingly.
  3. Simmer on low heat.
  4. Optional make kache qeeme ke kabab.
  5. Garnish with boiled eggs and bareek chips.
  6. Cut all vegetables n add oil in a pan.
  7. Saute garlic, then add chicken. Cook for awhile. Add normal pyaz, saute n then add capsicum, corn n gajar
  8. Let it cook. Once done add band gobhi.
  9. Fry garlic n in the leftover oil add chilli powder.
  10. For sauce: Mayonnaise Dahi Chilli flakes Tomato without skin Salt sugar Vinegar Tomato n chilli garlic sauce
  11. Blend Together
  12. Set the dish Rice with vegetables on top Put sage as well Kababs surrounding it Garnish with Hari pyaz n dhania
