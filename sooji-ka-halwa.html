Ingredients

  1. 900 gms maida
  2. 900 gms aata
  3. 2 packets wet yeast
  4. 2 cups oil
  5. 2 cups yoghurt
  6. 3 eggs

Preperation

  1. Mix all the above ingredients in the atta and maida and knead well.
  2. Keep aside for 45 minutes. Knead again and keep aside again for 15 minutes.
  3. Make 20 pedas roll them into naans and bake them in a prewarm oven at 275 degreesÂ centigrade.
