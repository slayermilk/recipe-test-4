Ingredients

  1. Left Over Biryani
  2. Curd
  3. Salt
  4. Green Chillies
  5. Coriander
  6. Onion, Sliced
  7. Tomato (optional)

Preperation

  1. Add curd, salt, green chillies, coriander and leave it overnight.
  2. Add water, 1 onion sliced, tomatoes (if want to) and let it cook.
  3. Switch off the flame, when the consistency is thinner.
