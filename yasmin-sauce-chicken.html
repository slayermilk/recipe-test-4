Ingredients

  1. Chicken 1kg
  2. Red Chilli Powder
  3. Haldi Powder
  4. Salt
  5. Pepper Powder
  6. Tandoori Masala 2 tbsp
  7. Ginger Garlic Paste
  8. Ketchup

Preperation

  1. Add the above ingredients to the chicken and boil till it’s almost done.
  2. Then add tomato ketchup about half bottle a bottle and little water.
  3. Simmer on low heat.
  4. Make tadka of zeera rai curry patta Kashmiri red chilli and lasan.
  5. Garnish with boiled eggs and bareek chips.
