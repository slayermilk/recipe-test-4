Ingredients

  1. Noodles 1 Packet
  2. Chicken, Small Pieces, 1kg
  3. 3/4 Onions Paste
  4. Lehsen
  5. 6/7 Cloves Barik
  6. Coconut Milk
  7. Besan
  8. Chilli Powder To Taste
  9. Salt to Taste
  10. Oil
  11. Hari Pyaz
  12. Kothmir
  13. Lehsen Tala Hua
  14. Roti Tali Hui
  15. Birishta

Preperation

  1. Take atleast 2 tablespoon oil, add lehsen once heated, then add onion paste, add a little salt n chilli powder too.
  2. Saute till pyaz becomes pinkish, add chicken with adrak lehsen, chilli powder and salt.
  3. Let it cook for awhile then add coconut milk n bring it to boil.
  4. Then add water according to the gravy you want.
  5. Let it boil n add besan mixed with water.
  6. Lower the flame and let it cook. Do not cover fully. Taste salt n mirch and add more if needed.
  7. Check the gravy, add more besan if not thick enough.
  8. Prepare the garnish. I use the oil of the Tala hua lehsen by adding chilli powder for the chilli sauce.
